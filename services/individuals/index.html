Individuals

We offer team-oriented coaching that enables leaders to maximize team performance and members to maximize contributions.

As a startup grows, a single team becomes multiple teams and each team gets its own leader or manager. Startups often fall into trap of promoting people who are not yet ready to be a manager.

Here are some examples of people who get promoted. Those who are well-liked but not familiar with what their direct reports do. Those who are great at their jobs so direct reports get assigned to them. Neither of these help boost team performance and often lead to frustration.

It may be tempting to adopt the management approach of large, traditional organizations because it is common and easy to implement. But a supervisory approach, designed for managing factories, is detrimental to startups.

Startups must develop managers who can become coaches. A coach who helps its team members do their best work. Coach-like managers maximize individual productivity by creating workflows, developing skills and mentoring.

An organization with coach-like managers has many benefits. Employees are fully aware of their development opportunities and can visualize career progression. They have trusted people in the organization who want them to succeed and will help them get there.

How we can help

We provide coaching to individual contributors and managers on a range of topics, including:

  • Goal setting,
  • Problem solving,
  • Emotional intelligence,
  • Productivity,
  • Communication,
  • Building trust,
  • Facilitating and participating in meetings,
  • Recognizing and managing conflicts,
  • Giving and receiving feedback,
  • Coaching and developing others.
