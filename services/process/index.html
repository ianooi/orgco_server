Process

We develop modern talent management practices that motivate people to reach your organization’s objectives.

Today’s talent management practices have not caught up with the demands of both organizations and employees to drive performance. We believe a new blueprint is urgently needed for the modern, forward-thinking workplace.

Different organizations, industries and geographies may have different definitions of optimal talent management practices, however to meet today’s demands, our new guiding principles start from the following.

  • Horizontal promotion of employees.
  • Team and individual goal-setting is regular, every quarter or more frequently.
  • Performance measures for managers and leaders are beyond traditional metrics.
  • Reward for individuals include their team’s impact on business goals.
  • Individual evaluations promote continuous learning.
  • Feedback is multi-dimensional, two-way and continuous.
  • Evaluations are fair and transparent.

How we can help

Performance management. We design processes and systems that meet the demands of a modern, forward-thinking workplace using new guiding principles.

Career development paths. We map vertical and horizontal professional paths to facilitate greater career opportunities for employees and more flexibility for leaders to deploy and redeploy talent across the organization.
