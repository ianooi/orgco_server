Process

We create talent management practices that give teams a sense of purpose, encourage team work, and help them continuously improve and innovate. We map career paths for people so they become more well-rounded.

It’s proven that teams can achieve more than individuals can do alone and organizations are changing from hierarchical structures to a flatter, network of cross-functional teams. However, getting the benefits of a team-based model require more than just moving people around the organization. The way talent management practices are being tackled right now also need a radical change. The good news is, agile organizations that made the transition have seen significant improvement in performance.

Some of these new practices include:

  • Horizontal promotion of employees.
  • Regular goal-setting, every quarter or more frequently.
  • Performance measures for individuals promote team work and continuous learning.
  • Performance measures for managers and leaders go beyond traditional metrics.
  • Feedback is multi-dimensional, two-way and continuous.

How we can help

Performance management. We design performance management practices to meet the demands of a modern, progressive workplace.

Career development paths. We map vertical and horizontal development paths so people have more career opportunities and leaders have greater flexibility when deploying and redeploying talent across the organization.
