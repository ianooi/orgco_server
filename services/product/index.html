Product

We help product leaders find true product-market fit and design scalable growth machines that stay in sync with the customer.

The product team builds features, the customer buys capabilities. The product is the part of the business that customers want to pay for. It incorporates the capabilities that enhance the customer. As such, the best products enable customers to feel superpowered after obtaining these new capabilities.

Many product teams are designed to be development-oriented and discover that their product is hard to sell and unable to retain customers. To solve this, a product team must be cross-functional with a leader who understands each function’s role. The product is a true team effort involving every function whose work interacts with the customer including marketing, sales, success, development and operations.

When the product team gets this right, acquiring and retaining customers becomes much easier. This is what many define as product-market fit (PMF). Our definition of PMF is when a team has the right features and the right messaging to attract buyers, and the features are built so that the buyers get their desired outcome and continue buying.

Achieving product-market fit is the first of many challenges. For a startup to scale, it must achieve a series of PMFs because each target market has its own requirements. Once the growth from one market begins to decline, product leaders must redeploy those resources to another market and find PMF again. Fortunately, each subsequent PMF is easier than the last because of scale. This is how product leaders can create scalable growth machines that follow the customer.

How we can help

Vision and strategy. We can review your vision and strategy, and validate that the assumptions are aligned with the current and future expectations of buyers and users, and the realities of the competitive landscape.

Decision-making. We can review your key product decisions and assess if your decision-making process has been designed to manage feature releases and bug fixes as investments with opportunity costs.

Team and individuals. We can design product teams around your service offering, help you hire the right people in the right roles and create coaching plans to upskill current team members.
