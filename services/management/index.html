Management

We coach managers so they can get better outcomes from their teams, support team members on their career goals, and create a productive environment for everyone.

Most companies know that they need to recruit the brightest people, but they don’t always have a plan to develop them. As a result, as companies grow, a mistake often made is promoting people who are not ready to be managers.

Managers play a critical role in the success of an organisation and can have large ripple effect on the rest of the organisation. Equipped with the right skills and mindset, they can prioritise efforts around where value is; develop deep functional expertise, common tools and competencies; support team members on their career goals; and create an inclusive and productive environment for everyone.

How we can help

We provide coaching to high-potential individuals and new managers on the following areas:

  • Productivity and time management
  • Communication
  • Problem solving
  • Emotional intelligence
  • Building trust
  • Goal setting
  • Team collaboration
  • Facilitating and participating in meetings
  • Recognizing and managing conflicts
  • Giving and receiving feedback
  • Coaching and developing others
  • Servant leadership
