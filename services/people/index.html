People

We equip your managers with skills to develop others and provide an environment that allow them to get better outcomes from their teams.

People development is often not the first topic founders think about, but one that is critical to their business’ success. As a result, as the company grows, a common mistake often made is promoting people who are not ready to be managers.

According to research from Gallup, only 18 percent of managers demonstrate a high level of expertise in managing others, yet 70 percent of employee engagement depends on the manager.

A high-performing manager can have positive ripple effect on the rest of your organization. They ensure teams are effective at accomplishing business goals, support their members in reaching their career goals and drive a positive impact to your company’s culture.

How we can help

We provide coaching and host in-house workshops on the following topics for high-potential employees and new managers.

  • Productivity and time management
  • Communication
  • Problem solving
  • Emotional intelligence
  • Goal setting
  • Team collaboration
  • Facilitating and participating in meetings
  • Recognizing and managing conflicts
  • Giving and receiving feedback
  • Coaching and developing others
  • Servant leadership
