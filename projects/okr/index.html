Turning strategy into measurable goals recovered slowing growth

Customer churn due to fierce competition, SaaS company defended its market position by using goal setting tool, Objectives and Key Results (OKR) to align efforts across the company.

The challenge

A SaaS company, a first mover in its industry, was experiencing rapid growth for two and a half years. But its success also led to new competitors developing a similar product, some with even better features and services. As a result, early adopters started to churn and the company’s revenue was not growing at the rate their early sales had predicted. The company had expanded from 2 to 120 people since its founding and they needed alignment in the organization to turn things around.

The impact

Customer retention was top priority for the business. Through workshops and coaching, we introduced Objectives and Key Results (OKR), a goal setting tool to the company. Traditionally, customer retention had been the domain of the customer success team. With OKR implemented, everyone in the company was quickly aligned on the new focus. All departments, including Product Management, Marketing and Sales, focused on a single objective - increase customer retention. Every month, each team reviewed and measured its efforts against the set goals (Key Results) and iterated actions accordingly for the next period. Their relentless focus on customer retention helped the business recover from the slowing growth and defend its market position.
