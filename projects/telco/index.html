Goal alignment across company to recover slowing growth

Fierce competition caused customers to churn, SaaS startup defended its market position by using goal management tool, Objectives and Key Results (OKRs) to focus efforts across company.

The challenge

A SaaS startup, a first mover in its industry, was experiencing rapid growth for two and a half years straight. However, its success also led to new competitors developing a similar product, some with even better features and services. As a result, early adopters started to churn and the company’s revenue was no longer growing at the rate early sales successes had predicted. The company had expanded from 2 to 120 employees since its founding and they needed everyone to focus on the right things to turn the situation around.

The impact

Customer retention was top priority for the business. Through workshops and coaching, we rolled out the goal management tool, Objectives and Key Results (OKRs), across the company. Traditionally, customer retention had been the domain of the customer success team. With OKRs in place they aligned everyone in the company on the new focus quickly. All departments, including Product Management, Marketing and Sales, focused on a single goal, increase customer retention. Every two months, each team reviewed and measured their efforts in the previous period against set goals and iterated their actions accordingly for the next period. The relentless focus on customer retention across the company allowed the business to recover from the slowing growth and defend its market position.
