A blueprint for scaling

Initially, the lack of organizational design is manageable because the team is small and aligned. Then as it grows, first to one large team then many large teams, alignment suffers.

To avoid this trap, startups must create effective teams, align actions with goals and prioritize outcome over output. Helping leaders create their blueprint for scaling is what we do.
