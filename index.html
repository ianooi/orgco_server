Scale the agile way

As your business scale and grow its customer base beyond early adopters, your company itself needs to change as well. The uncertainty of the mainstream market is still ahead of you, so without a well thought-out plan or simply adopting the set-up of a large, traditional company can be the beginning of the end of your promising start-up.

Your start-up needs to become an agile company. One that can still respond with entrepreneurial speed, but do so with a much larger group of people. Creating that agility requires effective alignment of your corporate mission with the day-to-day operations. Executed by purpose-driven people empowered to drive continuous improvement and innovation.
