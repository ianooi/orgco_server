Scaling is no easy feat

As your company scale, your role as a founder and the company itself changes dramatically. Your company needs a new organizational structure that can manage the growth. You have to manage and develop people. You need to make sure your company’s strategies turn into actions by a much larger group of people. All while growing a product, is no easy feat.
